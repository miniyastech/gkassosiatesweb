Who We Are

A Firm Built on
Precision & Integrity

GK Associates is a trusted asset advisory and valuation firm based in Tamil Nadu, committed to delivering accurate, compliant, and timely valuation reports for banks, financial institutions, businesses, and individuals.

We specialise in property valuation, bank-grade reporting, technical site inspection, and market value assessment — providing clients with the clarity and confidence they need for sound financial decisions.

Led by a qualified valuation professional with extensive hands-on experience in property inspection, documentation analysis, and market assessment, GK Associates combines technical expertise with field-level verification and strict regulatory compliance in every assignment undertaken.
